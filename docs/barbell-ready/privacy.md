# Barbell Ready Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

Barbell Ready is a local-first iPhone app for preparing barbell warmups, plate math, rest timing, and workout setup notes.

## Data Collection

Barbell Ready does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, a backend service, cloud sync, paid APIs, coaching APIs, AI advice, or external data sources.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the barbell setup preferences, plate inventory preferences, warmup plans, workout notes, timers, and any local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

Barbell Ready does not need camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, location, or tracking permissions for its documented v1 scope.

If future versions add optional notifications for timers or reminders, iOS will ask for permission before notifications are enabled.

## Purchases

Barbell Ready may offer an optional Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Health And Safety Boundary

Barbell Ready is not medical advice, physical therapy advice, professional coaching, or a substitute for safe lifting instruction. You are responsible for choosing appropriate loads, checking equipment, and stopping if something feels unsafe.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
