# Barbell Ready Support

Barbell Ready is a local-first iPhone app that helps lifters prepare repeatable barbell warmups, plate math, rest timing, and setup notes before a workout.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Barbell Ready
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include private health information, medical history, personal identifiers, gym account details, payment information, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Barbell Ready is a planning and tracking utility, not medical, physical therapy, coaching, or safety advice. Always use safe lifting practices, check your equipment, and follow guidance from qualified coaches or medical professionals when needed.
