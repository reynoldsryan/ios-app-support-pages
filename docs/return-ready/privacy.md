# Return Ready Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

Return Ready is a local-first iPhone app for saving manually entered retailer return deadlines, label/packing notes, car drop-off status, and refund amounts you choose to track.

## Data Collection

Return Ready does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, retailer policy feeds, shipping-carrier feeds, barcode/QR scanning, OCR, camera/photo import, maps, location tracking, email/inbox import, package tracking, or external data sources for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the retailer names, item labels, return deadlines, refund amounts, physical next-step notes, return stage, Pro unlock state, and local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to Ryan Reynolds, the support pages repository, retailers, carriers, analytics providers, or any backend service by the app.

## In-App Purchases

Return Ready may include a Pro unlock through Apple's in-app purchase system. Apple processes purchases and may receive transaction information under Apple's own terms and privacy policy. The app only reads local StoreKit entitlement status to unlock Pro features.

## Support Requests

If you contact support through GitHub Issues, anything you choose to post there may be public. Do not include order numbers, tracking numbers, labels, account credentials, payment information, home addresses, or sensitive personal information.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The current version will remain available at this URL.
