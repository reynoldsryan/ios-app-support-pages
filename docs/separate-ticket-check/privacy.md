# Separate Ticket Check Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Separate Ticket Check is a local-first iPhone app for evaluating manually entered same-day separate-ticket travel timing and saving trips you choose to keep.

## Data Collection

Separate Ticket Check does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, live flight data, gate data, airline-policy feeds, airport data feeds, minimum-connection-time databases, delay feeds, maps, OCR, camera/photo import, location tracking, or external data sources for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the trip labels, arrival/departure times, friction flags, verdicts, checklists, saved trips, Pro unlock state, and local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Separate Ticket Check's documented v1 scope is local-first and does not require location, camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, calendar, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Separate Ticket Check may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Travel Boundary

Separate Ticket Check provides a conservative planning checklist based on the times and flags you enter. It does not verify airline, airport, immigration, customs, baggage, gate, delay, visa, or minimum-connection rules, and it does not guarantee that any connection will be possible.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
