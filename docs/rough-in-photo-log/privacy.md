# Rough-In Photo Log Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Rough-In Photo Log is a local-first iPhone app for creating practical rough-in photo records and proof-pack PDFs from job-site photos, fixture/room tags, and notes.

## Data Collection

Rough-In Photo Log does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, a backend service, cloud sync, OCR, CRM integration, invoice generation, or external APIs.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the jobs, imported or captured photos, room and fixture tags, notes, Pro unlock state, and generated proof-pack export history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

Rough-In Photo Log may request Camera access so you can capture job-site photos and Photo Library access so you can import existing job-site photos.

The app does not request microphone, contacts, location, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Rough-In Photo Log offers an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
