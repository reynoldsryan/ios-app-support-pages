# Class Pack Guard Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

Class Pack Guard is a local-first iPhone app for manually tracking boutique fitness class packs, paid credits, expiration dates, booking windows, late-cancel cutoffs, reminders, calendar exports, and pack history.

## Data Collection

Class Pack Guard does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, a backend service, cloud sync, studio sync, ClassPass/Mindbody integration, scraping, receipt parsing, OCR, email import, or external APIs.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the class packs, studio names, pack names, credit counts, expiration dates, booking-window rules, late-cancel cutoff rules, booked-class notes, reminder state, calendar-export choices, Pro unlock state, and local history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

Class Pack Guard may request notification permission when you choose to set local reminders. It may request Calendar access when you choose to export a booked class to your calendar.

The app does not request camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, location, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Class Pack Guard is planned to offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Manual Tracking Boundary

Class Pack Guard does not verify studio policies, sync with studio systems, book classes, cancel classes, or guarantee savings. You are responsible for checking official studio apps, emails, and policies before making booking or cancellation decisions.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
