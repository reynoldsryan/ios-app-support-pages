# Deal Check Groceries Support

Deal Check Groceries is a local-first iPhone app that helps shoppers compare today's shelf price against their usual staple price so sale tags and bulk packs are easier to judge.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Deal Check Groceries
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include payment details, grocery receipts, loyalty-account information, addresses, phone numbers, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Deal Check Groceries is a simple comparison utility. It does not provide live grocery prices, coupons, retailer inventory, OCR, receipt import, loyalty-card integration, nutrition advice, shopping delivery, or financial advice. You enter and manage the prices you want to compare.
