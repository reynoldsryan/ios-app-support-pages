# Deal Check Groceries Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Deal Check Groceries is a local-first iPhone app for saving grocery staple memories and comparing today's shelf price against your usual price.

## Data Collection

Deal Check Groceries does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, live retailer pricing, coupons, retailer integrations, OCR services, receipt import, camera scanning, or AI services for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the staple names, usual prices, package sizes, units, store labels, local comparison history, and Pro unlock state you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Deal Check Groceries does not need camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, local notifications, or tracking permissions for its documented v1 scope.

## Purchases

Deal Check Groceries may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Grocery Price Boundary

Deal Check Groceries helps compare prices you enter. It does not fetch or verify live store prices, coupons, inventory, or retailer offers.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
