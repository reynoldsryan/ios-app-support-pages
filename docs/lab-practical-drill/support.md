# Lab Practical Drill Support

Lab Practical Drill is a local-first iPhone app for making timed study drills from your own permitted lab-station photos or text-only prompts.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Lab Practical Drill
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include payment details, school login credentials, student IDs, instructor materials you do not have permission to share, patient information, medical records, or any private classroom/course content you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Lab Practical Drill is a personal study-practice tool. It does not provide built-in anatomy or course content, identify specimens, label photos with AI/OCR, verify course requirements, replace instructor guidance, provide medical advice, or connect to a school/LMS.

Use only materials you are allowed to photograph or reproduce for your own study.
