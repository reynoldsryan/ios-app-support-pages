# Lab Practical Drill Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Lab Practical Drill is a local-first iPhone app for creating timed study drills from permitted lab-station photos or text prompts.

## Data Collection

Lab Practical Drill does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, AI services, OCR services, course databases, school/LMS integrations, or remote content services for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the decks, station prompts, hidden answers, optional hints, optional permitted photos you import, drill results, missed-station history, free-tier counters, and Pro unlock state you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Lab Practical Drill may ask for Photos access when you choose to import a permitted station image. Imported images are stored locally in your study deck and are not uploaded by the app.

For its documented v1 scope, the app does not require microphone, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, local notifications, camera capture, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Lab Practical Drill may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Course Materials and Medical Boundary

Lab Practical Drill is a personal study tool. It does not provide built-in anatomy or lab content, identify specimens, verify answers, label images, provide medical advice, or replace instructor guidance. Use only material you are allowed to use for personal study.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
