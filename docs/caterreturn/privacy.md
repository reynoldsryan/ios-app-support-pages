# CaterReturn Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

CaterReturn is designed as a local-only utility.

## Data collection

CaterReturn does not require an account, backend, analytics service, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party data service. Return-card details you enter are stored locally on your device.

## Photos

If you choose to add or reference a handoff photo, the app uses the photo only for your local return-card workflow. Photos are not uploaded by CaterReturn.

## Purchases

CaterReturn may offer a one-time Pro unlock through Apple's StoreKit. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. CaterReturn does not receive your payment-card details.

## Sharing

If you use iOS sharing features, the text or image you choose to share is handled by the destination you select. CaterReturn does not send messages automatically.

## Data deletion

Because CaterReturn stores its app data locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
