# CancelProof Support

CancelProof is a local-first iPhone app for keeping manual cancellation proof, expected final charges, and check-back steps together in one place.

## What CancelProof does

- Stores cancellation confirmation notes and proof references locally on your device.
- Helps you remember what final charge to check and when to check it.
- Lets you mark a case as charge checked, in dispute, resolved, or archived.

## What CancelProof does not do

CancelProof does not cancel services for you, connect to providers, scan email, detect charges, verify proof, connect to banks, or replace official provider confirmations.

## Getting help

If you need help with CancelProof, contact the developer through the App Store support link for this app. Include:

- the screen where you got stuck,
- what you expected to happen,
- your iOS version,
- and whether the issue affects adding a card, restoring Pro, or viewing saved cases.
