# CancelProof Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

CancelProof is designed as a local-first utility for manually tracking cancellation proof and follow-up checks.

## Data collection

CancelProof does not collect personal data, analytics, tracking data, or usage data for the developer.

## Local app data

Information you enter into CancelProof, such as provider names, confirmation notes, expected final charges, check-back dates, proof references, and dispute notes, is stored locally on your device. The developer does not receive or access this information.

## Third-party services

CancelProof does not connect to banks, email accounts, service providers, cancellation services, OCR services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Purchases

If you buy or restore CancelProof Pro, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment details.

## Children’s privacy

CancelProof is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the App Store support link for CancelProof.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
