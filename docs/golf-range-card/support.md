# Golf Range Card Support

Golf Range Card is a local-only iPhone app for turning a range bucket into a clear practice card you can use between shots.

For support, contact:

`rsr.apps.ios@gmail.com`

Please include the app name, your iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

## Purchase Help

Golf Range Card Pro is a one-time in-app purchase. If you already bought Pro, use Restore Purchase in the app's Settings & privacy screen.

## Privacy

Golf Range Card does not require an account, backend, analytics, tracking, camera, microphone, location, or photo library access in the current v1.
