# Golf Range Card Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Golf Range Card is designed as a local-only iPhone utility.

## Data Collection

Golf Range Card does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data in the current v1.

Practice cards, range notes, miss-pattern notes, next-session adjustments, and saved history remain on your device.

## Purchases

Golf Range Card Pro uses Apple's StoreKit in-app purchase system. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

## Tracking and Analytics

Golf Range Card does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Accounts and Backend

Golf Range Card does not require an account and does not use a backend service.

## Permissions

Golf Range Card does not request camera, photo library, microphone, location, contacts, Bluetooth, or health permissions in the current v1.
