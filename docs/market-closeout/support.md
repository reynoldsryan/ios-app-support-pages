# Market Closeout Support

Market Closeout is a local-first iPhone app for market and pop-up vendors who want to close a booth before leaving the event: count cash, record card/POS totals, subtract fees, see deposit and over/short, save local history, and export a CSV summary.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Market Closeout
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include payment details, bank details, tax records, payroll details, customer information, vendor secrets, home addresses, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Market Closeout is a local closeout utility. It is not a POS system, bank product, accounting system, payroll tool, tax-preparation product, bookkeeping advice, financial advice, inventory system, customer database, or cloud service. Always verify business, tax, payroll, accounting, and cash-handling decisions with your own records and qualified advisors.
