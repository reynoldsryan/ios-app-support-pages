# Market Closeout Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026

Market Closeout is a local-first iPhone app for recording booth closeout math at markets and pop-up events.

## Data Collection

Market Closeout does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, AI services, POS integrations, bank integrations, accounting integrations, tax services, payroll services, OCR, customer databases, inventory systems, or staff accounts for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the booth names, event names, closeout dates, cash denomination counts, starting bank amount, expected cash sales, card/POS totals you type in, fee/expense rows, notes, local history, CSV export content, and Pro unlock state you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

For its documented v1 scope, Market Closeout does not require camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, local notifications, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Market Closeout may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## No Accounting, Tax, Payroll, Bank, or POS Advice

Market Closeout performs neutral local arithmetic from values you enter. It does not provide accounting, tax, payroll, bookkeeping, banking, legal, or POS advice and does not replace your records, advisors, POS reports, payment processor reports, bank records, or legal obligations.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data from the app settings screen or by deleting the app from your device.
