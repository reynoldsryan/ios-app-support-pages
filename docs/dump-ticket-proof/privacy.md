# Dump Ticket Proof Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Dump Ticket Proof is a local-first iPhone app for creating practical disposal-fee proof records and shareable PDFs from dump tickets, load photos, disposal location details, and job tags.

## Data Collection

Dump Ticket Proof does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, a backend service, cloud sync, OCR, accounting integration, route/load tracking integrations, or external APIs.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the proof records, ticket photos, optional load photos, disposal locations, fee amounts, weights, material notes, saved/recent job tags, local app events used only inside the app, Pro unlock state, and generated PDF/share history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

Dump Ticket Proof may request Camera access so you can capture ticket or load photos and Photo Library access so you can import existing ticket or load photos.

The app does not request microphone, contacts, location, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Dump Ticket Proof offers an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
