# Street Sweep Shield Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Street Sweep Shield is a local-first iPhone app for saving street-sweeping parking rules, move reminders, parking history, and notes you create.

## Data Collection

Street Sweep Shield does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, city parking data feeds, maps, OCR/sign recognition services, geofencing, location tracking, or external data sources for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the street/block labels, side-of-street choices, sweep days/times, reminder settings, notes, parking status, move history, Pro unlock state, and local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Street Sweep Shield's documented v1 scope is local-first and does not require location, camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Street Sweep Shield may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Legal And Parking Boundary

Street Sweep Shield helps organize your own reminders. It does not verify municipal parking rules, track holiday suspensions, monitor signs, file disputes, or provide legal advice.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
