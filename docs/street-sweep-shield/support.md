# Street Sweep Shield Support

Street Sweep Shield is a local-first iPhone app that helps you save street-sweeping parking rules, keep a visible move reminder, and log when you moved the car.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Street Sweep Shield
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include home addresses, exact parking locations, license plates, payment information, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Street Sweep Shield is a reminder and personal record utility. It does not provide legal advice, municipal parking-rule verification, holiday/suspension automation, city data feeds, maps, geofencing, sign recognition, ticket dispute help, or a guarantee that you will avoid a parking ticket. You are responsible for checking posted signs and local rules.
