# Material Proof Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Material Proof is a local-first iPhone app for documenting jobsite material check-ins and creating proof records before installation work starts.

## Data Collection

Material Proof does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, OCR services, barcode scanning services, supplier integrations, team sync, or external data sources for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the job names, material lines, issue tags, notes, photo placeholders or future local photo references, generated proof/export history, Pro unlock state, and local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Material Proof's documented v1 scope is local-first. If a version lets you capture or import photos, iOS will ask for camera or photo library permission before those features are used.

Material Proof does not need microphone, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, or tracking permissions for its documented v1 scope.

## Purchases

Material Proof may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Professional And Legal Boundary

Material Proof helps organize records. It does not provide legal advice, insurance advice, construction advice, supplier verification, or professional dispute-resolution guidance.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
