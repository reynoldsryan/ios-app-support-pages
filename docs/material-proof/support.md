# Material Proof Support

Material Proof is a local-first iPhone app that helps small install crews document owner- or GC-supplied materials before work starts, then export a clean proof record for damaged, opened, wrong, missing, or usable items.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Material Proof
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include private addresses, customer names, photos of people, payment information, supplier account details, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Material Proof is a documentation utility. It does not provide legal advice, insurance advice, construction advice, supplier verification, inventory auditing, or professional dispute-resolution guidance. You are responsible for reviewing the proof record before sharing it.
