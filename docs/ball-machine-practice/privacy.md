# Ball Machine Practice Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026

Ball Machine Practice is a local-first iPhone app for planning and running tennis and pickleball ball-machine practice sessions.

## Data Collection

Ball Machine Practice does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, AI services, coach marketplaces, shared drill libraries, machine-control services, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi/infrared machine integrations, or remote hardware services for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the sport choice, saved practice queues, drill details, session history, next-session adjustments, local machine-profile notes if added, and Pro unlock state you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

For its documented v1 scope, Ball Machine Practice does not require camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, local notifications, or tracking permissions.

## Purchases

Ball Machine Practice may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## No Hardware Control or Coaching Advice

Ball Machine Practice helps you organize practice. It does not control ball machines, diagnose equipment, verify safe machine operation, provide professional coaching advice, or replace your own judgment about physical activity, injury risk, court safety, or equipment setup.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
