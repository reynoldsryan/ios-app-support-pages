# Breaker Map Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-07

Breaker Map is a local-first iPhone app for recording a simple home breaker-panel map.

## Data collection

Breaker Map does not collect personal data, does not require an account, and does not send your breaker/circuit entries to a server in v1.

## Data storage

The information you enter is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be removed by iOS.

## Third-party services

Breaker Map does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking in v1.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact information listed on Ryan Reynolds' App Store developer page.
