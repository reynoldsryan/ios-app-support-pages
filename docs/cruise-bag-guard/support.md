# Cruise Bag Guard Support

Cruise Bag Guard is a local-first iPhone app that helps cruise passengers handle the final-night luggage handoff: bags that go outside the cabin, essentials that stay with you, and a morning pickup card.

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, privacy questions, or feature requests, open an issue here:

https://github.com/reynoldsryan/ios-app-support-pages/issues

Please include:

- app name: Cruise Bag Guard
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- app version
- a short description of what happened

Do not include passport numbers, booking numbers, cabin numbers, travel documents, payment information, photos of people, or anything you do not want public.

## Important Boundary

Cruise Bag Guard is an organization utility. It does not provide cruise-line instructions, customs or immigration advice, travel advice, safety guidance, live ship or port information, terminal maps, or official disembarkation rules. Always follow your cruise line's current instructions and posted ship guidance.
