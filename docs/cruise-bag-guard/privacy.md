# Cruise Bag Guard Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Cruise Bag Guard is a local-first iPhone app for organizing final-night cruise luggage handoff notes, keep-with-me essentials, local reminders, and a morning pickup card.

## Data Collection

Cruise Bag Guard does not collect personal data.

The app does not use accounts, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, backend services, cloud sync, paid APIs, cruise-line integrations, OCR services, AI services, live ship data, or external data sources for its documented v1 scope.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the trip names, luggage descriptions, tag details, pickup-group notes, keep-with-me checklist items, reminder choices, Pro unlock state, templates, export history, and local app history you create on your device using local iOS storage.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or shared with third parties by the app.

## Permissions

Cruise Bag Guard may ask for local notification permission if you choose to set a reminder. You can continue using the app without reminders.

Cruise Bag Guard does not need camera, photo library, microphone, contacts, location, health, motion, Bluetooth, local network, or tracking permissions for its documented v1 scope.

## Purchases

Cruise Bag Guard may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase if a paid version is configured. Apple processes purchases through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Travel Boundary

Cruise Bag Guard helps organize personal notes. It does not provide cruise-line instructions, customs or immigration advice, travel advice, safety guidance, or official disembarkation rules. Always follow your cruise line's current instructions.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
