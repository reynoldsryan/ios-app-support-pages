Colorist Formula Card Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

Colorist Formula Card is designed to keep formula-card information on your device. It does not require an account and does not provide cloud sync in v1.

Information you enter

You may enter nicknames, optional client details, formulas, notes, visit dates, photos, and local card metadata. Full client identity is optional.

Storage

App data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple platform storage. Deleting the app may delete local app data unless your device backup restores it.

Sharing and exports

If you choose to export or share a formula card, the exported content is handled by iOS share destinations you select.

Purchases

If Pro is enabled, purchases and restores are handled by Apple in-app purchase systems. The app does not receive your payment card details.
